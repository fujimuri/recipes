Roasted Cauliflower and Cauliflower Lentil Soup


Ingredients:
  • Cauliflower
  • 1/2 cup Red lentils
  • 1 bunch Cilantro or Parsley
  • Olive oil
Cooking directions:
  1. Wash the cauliflower and place in a pot. If you have a pot that fits the whole cauliflower, use it. If not, you can cut the cauliflower into a few chunks so that you can fit them all in the pot.
  2. Pour water into the pot until the cauliflower is submerged.
  3. Bring the pot to a boil. Add salt (about 1/2 tbsp) and reduce heat to low.
  4. Keep cooking until fork goes through cauliflower easily.
  5. Remove cauliflower from water. Do not discard the water in the pot!
  6. Wait for the cauliflower to cool a bit. Meanwhile, make a soup with the leftover yummy water: add 1/2 cup of red lentils and cook until they fall apart. Then add chopped cilantro, cook 5 more minutes and the soup is done. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Drizzle olive oil on your cauliflower, add salt and pepper to taste and put in an oven that was pre-heated to at least 200 c.
  8. Roast cauliflower until it gets golden brown spots, like in the picture. That can take a while, depending on the oven. Start checking after 20 minutes in the oven.
  9. That's it! You have a delicious roasted cauliflower and a flavorful soup.
